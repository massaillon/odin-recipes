Roasted Parmesan Green Beans.

Ingredients needed

  • 12 oz fresh green beans ; trimmed
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • fresh pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons shredded parmesan

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Spray 8x8-inch baking dish with cooking spray;

  2. Lay green beans out on a baking sheet and drizzle oil over them.

    Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder and toss evenly to coat.

  3. Spread them out of the sheet so they all lay flat and place on the lower third section of your oven.

  4. Bake for 10 minutes, shake the pan to turn; bake an additional 5 minutes.

    Remove from oven and sprinkle with grated cheese.
